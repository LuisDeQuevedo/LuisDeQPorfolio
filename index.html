Columbia Airplane Club

So far in the semester, I have designed the main fuselage component that connects the wing and the fuselage cross bars together. The piece gives the wings a dihedral design by tilting them by 3 degrees. This piece also acts as the plane's battery holder.

Senior Design

For my senior design, I am developing a robot that teaches children how to recycle by sorting objects. I designed the preliminary CAD model that was used to present our project direction to our professor and class. Our project will use image recognition software to identify and sort trash and recyclables. It will have a speaker that will educate people on what trash goes where and why, and it will also give feedback on how to be more environmentally friendly.
