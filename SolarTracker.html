Arduino Solar Tracker

In the scope of this project, I engineered a 360-degree rotating platform to serve as a solar tracker that effectively monitored and followed the most intense light source. This sophisticated tracking mechanism incorporated four precision photoresistors for seamless operation. With a well-coordinated combination of a stepper motor and a servo motor, my tracker exhibited the capability to dynamically orient itself to any position, thereby maximizing the efficiency of the solar panel's exposure to sunlight. The entire project was meticulously designed in SolidWorks and meticulously programmed using the Arduino IDE. Moreover, both the model and the wiring configuration were thoughtfully labeled to facilitate comprehensive documentation and to enhance the clarity of our project presentation.
