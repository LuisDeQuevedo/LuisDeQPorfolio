Machine Design

As part of Columbia University's Machine Design course (MECE 3409), I am developing a four-bar linkage system that is tasked with presseing a randomized sequence of colored buttons. I am working with four other brilliant engineering students to design, build, and test our mechanism.

The objective, as outlined by Professor Yesilevskiy, is to press the button pairs as quickly as possible. In order to execute this task, our mechanism must maintain a good transmission angle, keep a low volume, and minimize the weight of the coupler.
