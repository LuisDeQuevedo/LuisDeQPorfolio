Senior Design

For my Senior Design, I am working with three other students to develop a year-long mechanical solution to any engineering problem. We have decided to tackle the issue of the lack of awareness for proper recyling practices.

I have developed a preliminary concept in SoldiWorks in order to communicate our intended solution. SORT-E, our solution, will have an innviting face for children so that they will learn from it as it teaches about recycling. Users will be able to place any piece of trash on a conveyor belt so that SORT-E can pick it up with a mechanical robotic arm and deposite it in the appropriate bin. As it does that, it will tell children why that object belongs there.

We are currenlty working on the robotic arm by modeling and testing different gripper designs to optimize our project.
